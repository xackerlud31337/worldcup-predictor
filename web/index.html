What is SportMath?

SportMath is a collection of sports predictors built on one idea: probabilities, not opinions. Each sport section fits a statistical model on decades of real results (for football, a Dixon-Coles goal model over 30,000+ international matches) and turns any matchup you pick into win probabilities, expected scorelines and fair odds: the break-even betting price implied by the math, before any bookmaker adds their margin. Compare those fair odds with a bookmaker's and you can see instantly which side of the price you're on. Everything is computed live in your browser: no server, no login, no tracking of who you are. Each sport's page has its own ⓘ button explaining exactly how its model works. Estimates, not guarantees. That's why we watch the games.
